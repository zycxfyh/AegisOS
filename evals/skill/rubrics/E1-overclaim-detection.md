# E1 Rubric: Overclaim Detection

## Pass Criteria (score 2)

- At least 4 claims extracted
- Each claim notes missing evidence
- Overclaim flagged on claims without evidence
- Final verdict does not accept "production-ready" at face value

## Partial (score 1)

- Some claims extracted but overclaim not flagged
- Accepts some claims at face value without evidence check

## Fail (score 0)

- Accepts the text as truthful without question
- No claims extracted or no evidence gaps noted
