Ordivon does not exist first as a commercial product. Ordivon exists first as a
companion governance system for its creator.

Commercialization, public release, users, community, and company formation are
possible externalization paths. They are not the origin of Ordivon's meaning.

> Ordivon exists first as a companion governance system for its creator: to make
> decisions traceable, actions accountable, outcomes reviewable, knowledge

> Commercialization is an optional externalization, not the origin of its

## First Principles

Human life under uncertainty has a repeated structure:

```text
finite resources + uncertain world + consequential choices + delayed outcomes
```

The central problem is not only how to earn money, ship software, learn faster,
or use AI. The central problem is:

```text
How can a person make better choices over time,
understand why they chose,
observe what happened,
learn without self-deception,
and update their rules without losing themselves?
```

Ordivon is the system answer to that question.

## Companion Governance Loop

All Ordivon domains reduce to one loop:

```text
Intent
→ Constraint
→ Decision
→ Action
→ Evidence
→ Outcome
→ Review
→ Rule Update
→ Identity / System Evolution
```

The loop is not meant to mechanize life. It is meant to make consequential
action more observable, explainable, reviewable, learnable, and humane.

packs, not the whole system.

| Relationship / Social | trust, promises, boundaries, mutuality | Are my relationships respectful, truthful, and life-giving? |

These packs are future governance domains. They should not all be implemented at
once. The system must grow from real use and receipts, not from fantasy
taxonomy.

## Five System Layers

| Layer | Question | Objects |
|-------|----------|---------|
| L1 Record | What happened? | events, changes, decisions, receipts |
| L2 Explanation | Why did it happen? | intent, context, assumptions, constraints |
| L3 Review | What resulted? | outcome, deviation, luck, error, execution quality |
| L4 Rule | What changes next time? | CandidateRule, discipline, thresholds, warnings |
| L5 Self Model | Who am I becoming? | capability, recurring bias, values, direction |

Ordivon is not only optimizing a decision. It is helping improve the decision
maker.

## Anti-Mechanization Boundary

If Ordivon is badly built, it can become over-recording, self-judgment,
quantification anxiety, control obsession, or productivity theater.

Therefore Ordivon must preserve non-governed human space:

- rest
- play
- aesthetic experience
- curiosity
- friendship
- love
- inspiration
- silence
- unmeasured experience

The system should help the creator become freer, not tighter; clearer, not more
anxious; more honest, not more self-punishing; more able to act, not more
addicted to analysis.
