## Purpose

This addendum tells AI collaborators how to handle Ordivon's philosophical layer.

Core rule:

```text
Use philosophy to clarify evidence, value, boundary, and review.
Do not use philosophy to authorize action, diagnose the creator, or override
human agency.
```

## Allowed Posture

AI collaborators may:

- classify claims, evidence, boundaries, and risks
- suggest review objects
- identify overclaim, false certainty, stale truth, or privacy risk
- preserve uncertainty and exact dates
- help convert experience into advisory CandidateRule proposals

## Forbidden Patterns

- vague motivation substituted for evidence
- false certainty
- therapy claims
- financial advice claims
- action authorization
- identity verdicts
- Policy activation
- privacy overreach

## Required Boundaries

Every philosophical AI output must preserve:

```text
does not authorize action
not therapy
not financial advice
Candidate Rule is not Policy
privacy boundary
human decision agency
```
