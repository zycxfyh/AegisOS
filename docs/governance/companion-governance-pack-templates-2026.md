## Purpose

Ordivon's root meaning is companion governance first and external product wedge
second. Companion packs should help the creator make decisions traceable,
actions reviewable, outcomes learnable, and rules updateable without turning
life into hard productivity machinery.

## Universal Minimal Loop

Each pack records one small loop:

```text
Decision or Action:
Evidence:
Outcome:
Review:
Rule Update or No-Rule Rationale:
Boundary:
```

The default output is reflection and better next action, not scoring.

## Pack Templates

### Body / Energy

Question: does my body support the next meaningful work?

Evidence examples:

- sleep.
- fatigue.
- training.
- food.
- energy window.

Boundary: body signals cannot become hard productivity gates.

### Finance

Question: does this decision increase freedom or fragility?

Evidence examples:

- thesis.
- risk.
- maximum loss.
- position size.
- re-evaluation condition.

Boundary: Finance Pack is read-only and does not authorize trading.

### Learning

Question: did learning become usable ability?

Evidence examples:

- source.
- notes.
- output.
- skill transferred.
- next practice.

Boundary: information intake is not proof of competence.

### Builder

Question: did this build strengthen the system or add debt?

Evidence examples:

- changed files.
- tests.
- receipt.
- review.
- lesson.

Boundary: builder receipts do not approve merge, release, or deployment.

### Emotion

Question: is this emotion signal, noise, or unmet need?

Evidence examples:

- trigger.
- intensity.
- bodily state.
- decision impact.
- next gentle action.

Boundary: emotion review cannot become self-punishment or productivity scoring.

### Relationship

Question: did this interaction preserve respect, truth, and boundary?

Evidence examples:

- commitment.
- communication gap.
- repair needed.
- reciprocity.
- boundary.

Boundary: relationship pack is not surveillance and not a hard gate.

### Values / Constitution

Question: what principle should constrain this action?

Evidence examples:

- value at stake.
- tradeoff.
- non-negotiable.
- exception risk.
- future self impact.

Boundary: constitution statements constrain action; they do not erase evidence,
review, fallibility, or human judgment.

## Anti-Overforce Rule

If a pack makes the creator more anxious, more performative, or less free, it
must be simplified. The pack exists to create clarity and learning, not control
for its own sake.

## Boundary

Companion packs do not create medical, financial, legal, therapeutic, trading,
relationship, or life-direction advice for others. They are personal governance
templates and evidence loops only.
